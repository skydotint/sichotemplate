Founder Chairman

SICHO’s visionary founder chairman late Mr. S.I. Chowdhury, just after completion of higher education, started his business in the year 1968 under the name and style of S.I. Chowdhury & Co. Ltd. Since inception, he has been devoting his entire energy to build up this Company and it is only through his tireless efforts that this Company today is one of the pioneers in its own field. (S.I. Chowdhury & Co. Ltd.) SICHO with her sister concerns are now making a positive contribution towards the development of human resources and economic growth of Bangladesh. He has earned personal respect due to his cordial behaviour and extension of his sincere co-operation and valuable advice to his associates in all circumstances. He is not only engaged with the business community but is also associated with various charitable organizations. He is managing many Schools and Collages. The name of S.I. Chowdhury is not only popular in the business society but also with Educational and Social Bodies because of his active role in the social and religious activities.

Managing Director

Engineer A.M.M. Saiful Islam Saiful Islam Chowdhury was a student of Cadet College and obtained Engineering Degree from Surat University, India and he is actively participating in the management of SICHO. After the death of founder chairman, he undertakes all roles & responsibilities and started working as Managing Director of SICHO. His well behaviour in and out side the office have placed him in a very respectable position among the members of the Group, his colleagues & friends and members of the business associates. He is associated with various youth and social organizations for upgrading the society in literacy health care and sports. His vast experience and technical knowledge in handling odd sized and heavy weight cargo gives the company a clear edge over its competitors to undertake any challenge in the field of handling project cargo.

Director

Mr. A.S.M. Mainul Islam Chowdhury obtained B.Com (Hons.) degree from Aligarh Muslim University, India in the year 1994. Thereafter he obtained MIM (Masters of International Management) from Bond University, Australia in the year 1996 and also obtained Post Graduate Diploma in Information Technology from University of Technology Sydney in the year 2006. His double major in Business and IT will help him to move SICHO ahead keeping in pace the modern world strategy and technology. He is responsible for International marketing and liaising with SICHO’s foreign partners as well as setting up the strategy for Global Operations and plays active role in the planning of SICHO’s future business expansion.

Director

Mr. A.N.M. Shahidul Islam Chowdhury has accomplished BBA (Bachelors in Business Administration) duly from North South University, Dhaka in the year 1997 and thereafter MBA (Masters in Business Administration) from University of Coventry, UK in the year 1999. Having immense experience in the field of C&F business, he is well conversant about Customs Tariff, Clearing & Forwarding procedure & Rules and Regulations of National Board of Revenue (NBR).
